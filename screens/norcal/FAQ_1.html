File Formats

What file types do you accept?

For the highest precision, we prefer vector files (.AI, .EPS, or .PDF). For raster images like high-res logos, we require a minimum of 300 DPI at full print size.

Do you offer digitizing services?

Absolutely. We convert your artwork into stitch-ready files in-house to ensure every thread path aligns with our quality standards.

inventory_2

Orders & Minimums

Is there a minimum order quantity?

Our standard minimum for custom embroidery is 12 units. For large-scale precision prints, we typically start at 24 units per design.

Can I provide my own garments?

To ensure the integrity of our craftsmanship, we prefer to source from our curated catalog of premium textiles, but we review client-provided items on a case-by-case basis.

schedule

Lead Times

How long does an order take?

Our standard production window is 10-14 business days following final proof approval. During high-demand seasons, this may extend slightly.

Do you offer rush production?

Yes. We reserve a limited number of "Precision Slots" each week for urgent needs. Please contact us directly for availability and rush fees.

Still have questions?

Our studio team is available Monday through Friday for detailed consultations on your specific project needs.
