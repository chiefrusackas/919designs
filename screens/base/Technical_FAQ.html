Preferred Vector

We require .AI (Adobe Illustrator) or .EPS files with all fonts converted to outlines. Stroke weights must be a minimum of 0.5pt for precision milling.

Raster Assets

High-resolution .TIFF or .PSD files at 300DPI minimum at 1:1 scale. No compressed .JPG formats for master production assets.
