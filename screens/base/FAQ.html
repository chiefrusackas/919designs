For embroidery, we require digitized files in .DST or .PES format. For DTF printing, please provide vector-based .AI, .EPS, or high-resolution .PNG (300 DPI minimum) with transparent backgrounds. If you don't have these, our technical team can assist with digitization for a flat conversion fee.

We pride ourselves on scalability. For DTF transfers, there is NO MINIMUM. For complex embroidery projects, we recommend a minimum of 12 units to optimize machine setup time and provide you with the most competitive pricing.
