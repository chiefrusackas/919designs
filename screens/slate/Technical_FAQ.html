01. Materiality & Integrity

What is the GSM specification of the Monolith Hoodie? expand_more
Our Monolith Hoodie utilizes a 600 GSM ultra-heavyweight brush-back fleece. The yarn is 100% organic long-staple cotton, custom-dyed and pre-shrunk via an industrial heat-setting process to ensure zero dimensional shift post-purchase.
Describe the "Dry Hand" finish. expand_more
Unlike standard silicon-treated garments that feel artificially soft, our "Dry Hand" finish relies on mechanical abrasion during the knitting process. This creates a textured, architectural drape that ages with a unique patina relative to the wearer's environment.

Standard Benchmarks

  • 380-600 Grams Per Square Meter
  • Double-Fold Flatlock Seam Reinforcement
  • 12-Gauge Precision Knit Complexity

02. Distribution Logic

International fulfillment timelines? expand_more

All units are shipped via expedited industrial logistics. North American transit: 3-5 cycles. International: 7-12 cycles depending on regional clearing protocols.

Custom order synchronization? expand_more

Custom commissions enter a separate fabrication queue. Lead times are calculated based on machinery availability and complexity of the embroidery matrix.

03. Ecological Ethics

What is the Lifecycle Guarantee? expand_more

We design for a 10-year minimum operational lifecycle. If a seam fails under normal utilitarian conditions, we provide repair kits or internal restructuring services.

Water conservation metrics? expand_more

Our finishing plant utilizes a closed-loop filtration system, recycling 98% of all water used in the dyeing process, meeting ISO 14001 environmental standards.
