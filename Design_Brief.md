# Project: 919designs - "Iron & Thread" Edition
## Design Handoff Document

### 1. Project Overview
919designs is a high-end embroidery and DTF printing company specializing in technical apparel for professionals (firefighters) and enthusiasts (motocross). The brand identity, "Iron & Thread," reflects technical precision, industrial craft, and a subtle heritage of service.

### 2. Visual Identity & Design System
- **Core Aesthetic:** "The Precision Engine" — Clean, efficient, and industrial. It rejects generic "tactical" tropes for a more refined, engineering-focused look.
- **Color Palette:**
    - **Primary Background (Slate):** #506673 (Rich, industrial blue-gray)
    - **Surface/Containers:** #131313 (Deep matte black)
    - **Accent/Action:** #912825 (Deep oxblood red — inspired by firefighting heritage)
    - **Typography/Details:** #EEE7D4 (Cream/Bone — for high contrast and legibility)
- **Typography:** **Space Grotesk**. A geometric, tech-leaning sans-serif that conveys modern craftsmanship and precision.
- **Shapes:** Sharp, square corners to maintain the industrial, "machined" feel.

### 3. Screen Inventory & Flow
1. **The Catalog (The Catalog - Iron & Thread Edition):** A structured gallery showcase for headwear, base layers, and outer shells.
2. **Intake Form (Start Your Build - Iron & Thread Edition):** A multi-step technical specification form for client intake (Category, Dimensional Data, Visuals, Project Narrative).
3. **Technical FAQ (Technical FAQ - Iron & Thread Edition):** A clean, accordion-style documentation page covering materiality, distribution, and ethics.

### 4. Technical Implementation Notes
- **Hosting:** Cloudflare Pages
- **Codebase:** HTML/Tailwind CSS
- **Framework:** Vanilla HTML/JS with Tailwind for styling.
- **Assets:** High-resolution product photography with desaturated, high-contrast editing to match the slate/oxblood theme.
